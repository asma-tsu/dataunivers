Default analysis modules
These modules will be pre-selected each time you start a new analysis. You can always adjust them per document.
Academic Standards Check
Verifies compliance with APA, MLA, IEEE, Harvard, and your institution's templates.
Citation Analysis
Detects missing references, evaluates citation quality, and flags inconsistencies.
AI Content Detection
Estimates the probability of AI-generated sections and highlights flagged passages.
Plagiarism Screening
Identifies suspicious similarities across global academic databases.
Grammar & Writing Quality
Evaluates grammar, style clarity, and academic register.
Methodology Validation
Reviews the soundness and consistency of your research methodology section.
Research Structure Analysis
Checks overall structure, argument flow, and chapter organization.
Formatting Verification
